CTDA Internship

Connecticut Digital Archive Internship - Summer 2016

Live Site   –   Git Repo

CTDA manages a central archive for the smaller historical societies in CT that would otherwise be unable to have a digital presence due to the lack of funding. During my internship with CTDA I established their branding by starting a style guide and creating marketing materials such as new logo, bookmarks, outreach flyers, and a website. Throughout the internship I gained experience managing projects for clients who had a real stake in the projects.
