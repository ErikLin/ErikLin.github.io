Facility Throughput Map

Pitney Bowes

Overview

The app was designed to make high error rates and equipment problems immediately visible for users to take action. The map view allows users to zoom in and tap to reveal more layers of detail. There are also multiple views that cover different aspects of facility operations. The real time visibility gave the operations and industrial engineering teams the data they needed to efficiently manage their facility.

Results

  • Reduced response times
  • Enabled real time visibility into facility KPIs
  • Enabled more effective resource management
