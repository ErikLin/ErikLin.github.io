NexShip Exception Wizard

Pitney Bowes

Overview

The facility QA team uses NexShip to process package exceptions. Along a with a junior designer I lead the initiative to create a “wizard” for more complex exception workflows. Previously associates had to go through 2-3 weeks of training to identify all the different exceptions and learn the various workflows, which sometimes required submitting Jira tickets. With this new guided experience many of the issues that previously went to a higher level support team via Jira could now be handled right away, reducing total processing time from days to seconds. Also, training time was reduced to just 1 week.
